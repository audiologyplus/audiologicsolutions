Welcome to Audiologic Solutions

At Audiologic Solutions, the success of our solutions lies in our combination of compassionate care, cutting-edge technology, and extensive experience. We are a locally owned practice in Rensselaer, Hudson & Queensbury, NY are proud to offer comprehensive hearing healthcare services to the community

Our offices focus on making you feel comfortable by providing quality care with a compassionate touch. We honor the needs and priorities of each patient as an individual, and never try to create a “one size fits all” approach. At Audiologic Solutions, we know your hearing loss affects you differently than anyone else – so it’s our promise to create hearing solutions that work best for you.

Whether you’re in need of a complete hearing evaluation, have a question about your current hearing aids, or find that tinnitus is taking away from your ability to enjoy each day, we’re here to help.
